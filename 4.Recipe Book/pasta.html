Pasta Recipe

Back to Recipe Book

Ingredients:

    ```html
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian herbs (basil, oregano, thyme)
  • 1 cup chopped vegetables (such as bell pepper, zucchini, or mushrooms)
  • 1/2 pound ground meat (beef, turkey, or chicken) or plant-based protein
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Optional: red pepper flakes for heat
    • ```

Instructions:

    ```html
  1. If using, in a separate skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped vegetables and sauté for 5-7 minutes until tender.
  3. If using ground meat, add it to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon, about 7-10 minutes.
  4. Season the sautéed mixture with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and Italian herbs.
  5. Add the sautéed vegetables and meat to the pot of pasta and sauce, stirring to combine everything evenly.
  6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh basil and additional Parmesan cheese, if desired.
  8. For extra flavor, sprinkle with red pepper flakes before serving, if using.
    9. ```
